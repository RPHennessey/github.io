What is TeamMate?

  • Whitepaper
  • Executive Summary
  • Bounty Program

TeamMate is a global fitness network created to solve the triple threats of obesity, physical inactivity, and loneliness. Our technology aims to bring people together in real life, which means that we need to look at social networks in an entirely different way.

Current social networks that focus on digital interaction have been shown to exacerbate these problems, while in-person, fitness -focused interaction is demonstrably the single healthiest thing a person can do.

The TeamMate Network aims to integrate the health, fitness and wellness industries into a unified blockchain ecosystem, which will make business truly efficient, transparent, and reliable. Data in this ecosystem will be owned and monetized by users, and data transfers will be quick, painless and secure.

The TeamMate App apps

In order to help facilitate real-life interaction, TeamMate is building a fitness-oriented mobile application to connect athletes to each other. The TeamMate app and the TeamMate Network will allow users to discover, share, discuss, and explore all aspects of their athletic journeys. Data generated form this process will be owned and monetized by users at their discretion.

With the TeamMate beta live, users can opt-in to alpha testing during the crowdsale. The full application will be loaded with a variety of features including but not limited to:

  • Tipping within the app and sponsoring content though the Conversations feature
  • Compensation for user-created data & sponsored content, purchased by health, fitness and wellness companies
  • Ability to purchase goods and services using $TMT on the TeamMate Marketplace
  • Staking $TMT as prizes for sponsored sporting events or competitions
  • Ability for gyms to incentivize new memberships with $TMT Token discounts or givebacks.

What is TeamMate?

TeamMate is a gamified, data-centric fitness application that encourages real-life participation through $TMT tokens. The TeamMate application allows trainers to sell goods and services on the TeamMate Marketplace along with the ability for individuals to monetize their fitness data through opt-in research campaigns.

What makes TeamMate unique?

TeamMate is the first fitness platform to utilize blockchain technology. TeamMate aims to capitalize on data as a vital sign by integrating healthcare services into the fitness-oriented platform. In time, TeamMate will serve as a global database to connect local and corporate fitness facilities along with providing a platform for individuals to organize intramural sports leagues and meet new workout partners compatible with their current workout routine.

What is the Token Release Contract?

The Token Release Contract is a program created to reward early participants. ICO participants will have the ability to lock their tokens in exchange for monthly bonus $TMT tokens. All originally staked tokens will be released equally over the course of 12 months on top of all bonus tokens received. The more $TMT you stake, the more bonus token you’ll earn. Further literature surrounding this program can be found in the official whitepaper.
